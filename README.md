BUDS is a free, web-based platform designed to connect volunteer educators, learners, and universities within a single unified system. The platform enables learners to access free educational support, allows volunteers to teach or mentor, and provides institutions with a reliable way to track and verify volunteer hours.
The goal of BUDS is to promote community learning, accessibility, and official recognition of volunteer contributions.
